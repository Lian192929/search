At iTz Search, we take your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we collect when you use our search engine, how we use that information, and how we keep it secure.

Information We Collect

When you use iTz Search, we collect the following types of information:

  • Search Queries: When you type a query into the search box, we collect that query and use it to provide search results. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or physical address.
  • Google API: Our search engine uses the Google API to provide search results. As a result, Google may collect information about your use of our search engine, in accordance with their own privacy policy.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to improve the quality of our search engine and to provide you with the best possible search results. We do not sell or share your information with third parties, except as required by law.

Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include SSL encryption for all data transmitted between your device and our servers, regular software updates, and restricted access to our servers.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or in applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes to this policy by posting the new policy on our website.
